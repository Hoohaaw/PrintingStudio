Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about getting your order and what to do if something goes wrong.

Shipping Rates & Times

Order Value Shipping Rate Estimated Delivery
Under $50 $5.99 flat rate 3–7 business days
$50 and over Free 3–7 business days
International From $14.99 7–21 business days
Express (domestic) From $12.99 1–2 business days

Delivery times are estimates and start from the dispatch date, not the order date. Most items are printed and dispatched within 3–5 business days. During peak periods, dispatch may take slightly longer — we'll always keep you informed.

Print & Dispatch Times

Every item at PrintForge is printed fresh to order — we don't hold shelf stock. Here's a typical timeline:

  • Order received — we confirm via email within a few hours
  • Day 1–3 — your item is printed and goes through our quality check
  • Day 3–5 — packed, labelled, and handed to the courier
  • Day 5–12 — delivered to your door (domestic estimate)

You'll receive an email with a tracking number once your order ships.

Packaging

We pack all orders in protective foam-lined boxes or padded mailers depending on size. Fragile items receive extra bubble wrap. We use recycled and recyclable materials wherever possible. If you need gift wrapping or a personalised note, just mention it in the order notes on the contact form.

Returns & Refunds

Defective or Wrong Item

If your item arrives damaged, defective, or is not what you ordered, contact us within 14 days of delivery. We'll either:

  • Reprint and reship the correct item at no charge, or
  • Refund the full order amount including shipping

Please include a photo of the issue in your email — it helps us improve.

Change of Mind

Because items are printed on demand, we can't accept returns for change of mind. However, we want you to be happy — if you're not 100% satisfied for any reason, contact us and we'll do our best to find a solution, whether that's a reprint in a different colour or store credit.

Custom Orders

Custom prints are non-refundable once printing has begun, unless they arrive defective. We recommend approving a detailed brief before we start to avoid any misunderstandings.

How to Start a Return

  1. Email us at hello@printforge.com with your order details
  2. Include photos if the item is damaged or wrong
  3. We'll respond within 24 hours with next steps
  4. Do not return items without prior authorisation — unauthorised returns cannot be processed

Cookies & Privacy

We use cookies solely to save your cart and remember your theme preference. We don't use tracking cookies or share your data with third parties. By using the site you agree to this minimal use of local storage. You can clear your browser data at any time to remove stored preferences.
