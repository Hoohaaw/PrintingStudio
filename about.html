About PrintForge

Passionate about precision, obsessed with quality — we're a small team of makers bringing 3D printing to everyone.

Our Story

Started in a Garage.
Grown into a Workshop.

PrintForge started in 2021 when our founder picked up a second-hand FDM printer and couldn't put it down. What began as a hobby — printing replacement parts and quirky figurines for friends — quickly became something bigger.

Today we run a fully-equipped workshop with multiple printers running around the clock. We serve collectors, tabletop gamers, designers, and anyone who needs a custom part printed with care.

Every item that leaves our shop is inspected by hand. We care about the details — from the first layer to the last.

What We Stand For

Quality, transparency and a genuine love for making things.

Precision First

We calibrate our printers daily and use only verified filament brands to guarantee consistent results on every print.

Made with Care

Every item is inspected before shipping. We sand, prime, or finish prints where needed — not just box them up.

Eco-Conscious

We use PLA wherever possible — a biodegradable plant-based plastic. Offcuts and failed prints are recycled, not trashed.

Always Responsive

Have a question about your order? We reply to every message within 24 hours, usually much faster.
