Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our prints, process, and policies.

Printing & Materials

We primarily use PLA, PLA+, and PETG. PLA is great for detailed figurines and display pieces. PLA+ offers extra strength and a slightly smoother finish. PETG is heat-resistant and UV-stable — ideal for functional or outdoor items. Each product listing specifies the material used.

Our standard filaments are non-toxic, but FDM prints have small layer lines and pores that can harbour bacteria — we do not certify them as food-safe. Articulated toys and decorative items are safe for older children (8+) with adult supervision, but are not designed for young children who may chew on small parts.

Layer height varies by product. Detail items (figurines, decorative pieces) are printed at 0.1–0.15 mm for the sharpest results. Functional parts are often printed at 0.2 mm for a balance of speed and strength. The exact layer height is listed in each product's specifications.

Shipping & Returns

Most items are printed and dispatched within 3–5 business days. Delivery time depends on your location. Domestic orders typically arrive within 2–4 days after dispatch. International shipping can take 7–14 days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Yes — orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. Orders under $50 have a flat shipping fee of $5.99. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

We offer free returns within 14 days of delivery if the item is defective or not as described. If you simply change your mind, we'll reprint it in a different colour or offer store credit. Contact us first before returning anything — we'll sort it out. Full details on our Shipping & Returns page.

Custom Orders

Absolutely — custom orders are our favourite! You can either send us an STL file or describe what you need via our Custom Order form. We'll respond with a quote within 24 hours.

We accept STL, OBJ, and 3MF files. STL is the most common format exported by Tinkercad, Blender, Fusion 360, and most other 3D design tools. If you're unsure, send us what you have and we'll let you know.
